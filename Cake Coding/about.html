About Cakes Galore

Cakes Galore, a page about cakes of all shapes and sizes, educates people of all ages on the types of cakes you can find in different aspects of life.

With images of both multilayered, and single layered cakes every visitor leaves our page with a watering mouth. We aim to entertain the cake fanatics with mouthwatering images of beautiful cakes.
